FA-Tshirt is not just a brand, it's a journey. Launched from the heart of New Ashok Nagar, this startup is driven by a passion for fashion, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

With a fire to build India’s #1 budget-friendly quality clothing brand, we offer styles for everyone. Whether you're from a small town or a metro city, FA-Tshirt believes fashion should never be out of reach.

From a single laptop and a ₹50,000 investment, we've grown step by step – handling e-commerce operations, designing, and dreaming big. We’ve hustled from Flipkart listings to learning trading, MIS reporting, and mastering Excel just to keep pushing forward.

Our vision? To make FA-Tshirt a household name across India. From trading charts to t-shirt prints – every move we make is toward freedom, success, and style.
