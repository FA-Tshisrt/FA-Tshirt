About FA-Tshirt

FA-Tshirt is not just a fashion brand, it’s a movement started by a dreamer who began his journey from scratch. From working in MIS to managing inventory and listing across Flipkart, Amazon, Meesho, and more – our founder hustled through every step. With a passion for stylish clothing and a sharp eye for trading and business, FA-Tshirt was born to bring high-quality, budget-friendly fashion to everyone. We believe in affordable fashion with premium quality, building India’s No.1 clothing brand for the youth.

With experience in Excel automation, trading analysis, and startup spirit, FA-Tshirt is growing to offer not just clothes, but confidence.

Dream: To make FA-Tshirt a globally loved brand starting from the streets of India.
